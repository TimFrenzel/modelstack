# Ideas / Backlog

Stuff we want to build or find. If something here looks interesting, grab it and open a PR. Or add your own ideas by editing this file and submitting a pull request.

Nothing here is assigned. Nothing is promised. It's just a list.

---

## Personal Finance

- Monthly budget tracker with rollover (unspent money carries into next month)
- Annual tax estimator that handles W-2 + 1099 income together
- Savings goal tracker with a visual progress bar per goal
- Subscription tracker... how much am I actually spending on apps every month?
- Net worth tracker that pulls from multiple account types (checking, brokerage, crypto, property)
- Emergency fund calculator based on actual monthly expenses, not some generic "3 months" rule

## Investing

- Roth vs Traditional IRA comparison with tax bracket inputs
- Dividend income tracker with yield-on-cost and projected annual income
- Options wheel strategy log (sold puts, covered calls, assignment tracking)
- Stock portfolio rebalancer that shows drift from target allocation
- Bond ladder planner

## Business

- Simple P&L for a small business with monthly and YTD views
- Invoice tracker with aging (30/60/90 day buckets)
- Project cost estimator with a Gantt-style timeline... might be ambitious but would be cool
- Inventory tracker for a small e-commerce shop

## Real Estate

- Rental property cash flow analyzer (the classic: income minus expenses minus mortgage)
- House hack calculator for duplexes and triplexes
- Rehab budget tracker with line items and actuals vs estimates
- Mortgage comparison tool (15 yr vs 30 yr, different rates, points vs no points)

## Freelance and Side Hustle

- Freelancer quarterly tax estimator with safe harbor calculations
- Client project tracker with hours, rates, and invoiced amounts
- Side hustle income log that separates multiple gigs
- Expense tracker for delivery drivers (mileage, gas, phone, etc.)

## Education and Career

- Student loan repayment comparison (standard vs income-driven vs avalanche)
- Salary negotiation worksheet... total comp breakdown with equity, bonus, benefits
- Course planning sheet for college students tracking credits and prerequisites
- Certification cost tracker for professional development (CPA, CFA, PMP, whatever)

## Other

- Meal planning budget sheet. Yeah it's technically food but it's still a budget.
- Warranty and subscription expiration tracker... is that even finance? Close enough.
- Moving cost estimator
- Car ownership total cost calculator (payment, insurance, maintenance, depreciation)
