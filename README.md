# ModelStack

We're a loose group of people who happen to live in spreadsheets. Some of us work in finance. A couple teach. A few just like building things in Excel on weekends because... that's apparently a hobby now. We come from different industries and different skill levels, but we kept running into the same problem: every time someone needed a budgeting template or a portfolio tracker, the options were either a $15/month SaaS app or some generic sheet with clip art and a blue header row.

So we started making our own. First it was just emailing files around. Then a shared Google Drive that got messy fast. Eventually someone said "let's just throw these on GitHub" and here we are.

## What you'll find

Stuff we actually use day to day (or built for friends who asked):

- Monthly budgets and expense trackers
- Net worth dashboards
- Retirement planning calculators
- Debt snowball / avalanche payoff sheets
- Portfolio trackers and rebalancing tools
- Side hustle and freelance income logs
- Options strategy P&L sheets
- Business cost estimators and project trackers

Some of these started as quick tools for our own workflows. Others came out of Reddit threads or Slack conversations where someone said "I wish I had a spreadsheet for that." If it's useful and deals with money or business or planning in some way, it probably belongs here.

## How it's organized

Everything lives under `sheets/`, sorted into loose categories:

| Folder | What goes there |
|--------|----------------|
| [personal-finance/](./sheets/personal-finance/) | Budgets, savings trackers, net worth, debt payoff, tax estimators |
| [investing/](./sheets/investing/) | Portfolio trackers, options logs, rebalancing tools, dividend trackers |
| [business/](./sheets/business/) | P&L sheets, invoicing, inventory, project cost estimators |
| [real-estate/](./sheets/real-estate/) | Rental cash flow, mortgage comparisons, rehab budgets |
| [freelance-and-side-hustle/](./sheets/freelance-and-side-hustle/) | Income logs, client trackers, quarterly tax estimators |
| [education-and-career/](./sheets/education-and-career/) | Student loan calculators, course planning, salary negotiation tools |
| [other/](./sheets/other/) | Anything useful that doesn't fit neatly above |

Don't overthink the category. Pick the closest one. If it could go in two places, just pick one and we'll sort it out.

For the full browsable index, check [CATALOG.md](./CATALOG.md).

## Got an idea?

We keep a running list of sheets we want to build or find. Some are specific ("Roth vs Traditional IRA calculator with tax bracket inputs"), some are pretty vague ("something for tracking car maintenance costs?"). Take a look at [IDEAS.md](./IDEAS.md). If something catches your eye, grab it and open a PR. Or add your own ideas to the list.

You can also open a [GitHub Issue](../../issues) if you want to discuss something before building it.

## Who's behind this

No company. No org chart. Just a handful of people who kept sharing spreadsheets and decided to put them somewhere useful. The group includes folks who work in accounting and finance, a couple of educators who build sheets for their students, some software people who'd rather automate everything in Python, and a few hobbyists who genuinely enjoy a well-built VLOOKUP.

We add stuff when we have time. Sometimes that's every week, sometimes there's a gap. It's not a production schedule, it's a shared toolbox.

If you want to contribute, check out the [contributing guide](./CONTRIBUTING.md). Short version: fork, add your sheet, open a PR. We're not picky about polish.
