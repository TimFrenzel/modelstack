## Personal Finance

- [Annual Spending Review](./sheets/personal-finance/annual-spending-review/README.md) - Compare this year against last year, rank the biggest spending swings, and sketch out a next-year forecast.
- [Envelope Budget](./sheets/personal-finance/envelope-budget/README.md) - Fund virtual envelopes from biweekly paychecks and let rollover rules decide what carries forward.
- [Financial Command Center](./sheets/personal-finance/financial-command-center/README.md) - Pull accounts, cash flow, goals, and allocation into one household finance workbook.
- [Fifty Thirty Twenty](./sheets/personal-finance/fifty-thirty-twenty/README.md) - Sort spending into needs, wants, and savings, then check each month against the 50 30 20 split.
- [Net Worth Tracker](./sheets/personal-finance/net-worth-tracker/README.md) - Track monthly balances, longer-run net worth history, and milestone dates in one file.
- [Personal Balance Sheet](./sheets/personal-finance/personal-balance-sheet/README.md) - Keep a quarterly household balance sheet with statement-style sections and health ratios.
- [Refi Or Paydown](./sheets/personal-finance/refi-or-paydown/README.md) - Compare a refinance, extra principal, and investing the difference over the same hold period.
- [Roth IRA Conversion Ladder Planner](./sheets/personal-finance/roth-ira-conversion-ladder-planner/README.md) - Map out early retirement conversions year-by-year to dodge penalties and track taxes owed.
- [Savings Goal Planner](./sheets/personal-finance/savings-goal-planner/README.md) - Build an emergency fund from real expenses, then see how the rest of your savings goals move around it.
- [Zero-Based Budget](./sheets/personal-finance/zero-based-budget/README.md) - Assign every dollar before the month starts, log the spending, and measure the gap between plan and reality.
