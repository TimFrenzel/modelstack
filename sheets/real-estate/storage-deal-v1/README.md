# Storage Deal v1

**Category:** Real Estate

**Summary:** Got a flyer for a small storage facility? This is the sheet I pull up to quickly figure out if the numbers make sense. I built this for buyers who want to test a lease-up story under new management, but don't want to get bogged down in institutional math. It's a practical scratchpad for a 5-year hold.

**Structure:** The Facility Overview tab handles your purchase price, loan terms, and basic lease assumptions. The real work happens on the Unit Mix & Lease-Up tab, where you can plug in up to six unit types (like 10x10s or parking) and run a 24-month schedule to build your effective gross revenue. Over on the Operating Costs tab, fixed lines step up each year, while management fees tie straight to your income. The Returns & Snapshot tab wraps it all into a single page so you know your exit equity multiple and levered IRR.
