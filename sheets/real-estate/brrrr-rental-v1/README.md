# BRRRR Rental v1

**Category:** Real Estate

**Summary:** Working on a BRRRR deal but aren't sure if the refinance will pull out enough cash? I built this sheet for investors who need to run the math on the buy, the rehab and the refi in one practical place. It's a solid tool for making sure your new debt payment doesn't kill your cash flow before you even close.

**Structure:** The Deal Setup tab tracks your purchase price, bridge debt and permanent loan assumptions so you can quickly see your total basis. In the Rehab Budget tab, you can itemize your scope and map out a simple 6-month draw schedule. The Stabilized Rental tab covers your operating expenses and calculates your NOI once the property is fixed up. The Refi & 5-Year Hold tab runs your DSCR. The BRRRR Snapshot gives you a clean one-page view of equity creation and cash left in the deal.
