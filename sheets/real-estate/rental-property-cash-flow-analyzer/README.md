# Rental Property Cash Flow Analyzer

**Category:** Real Estate

**Summary:** I put together this cash flow analyzer to figure out if a rental property actually makes sense. It's useful when you don't want to get surprised by hidden costs. Good fit for a single-family or small duplex.

**Structure:** Start on the 'Deal Inputs' tab where you drop in the purchase price, down payment and expected monthly rent. Then flip over to the 'Monthly Cash Flow' tab. It pulls all those numbers together, runs the math on your operating expenses and shows exactly what's left over at the end of the month.
