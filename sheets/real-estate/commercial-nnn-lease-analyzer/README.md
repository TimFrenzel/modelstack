# Commercial NNN Lease Analyzer

**Category:** Real Estate

**Summary:** I put together this commercial lease analyzer to figure out exactly what your effective rent is over the life of a Triple Net (NNN) lease. It breaks down base rent, CAM charges and property taxes so you know your true total obligation before signing any paperwork.

**Structure:** On the first tab, you input your overall lease terms, square footage and the landlord's estimated pass-through expenses. The output table then projects your total monthly and annual costs year-by-year, automatically factoring in standard periodic rent escalations so there are no surprises at renewal.
