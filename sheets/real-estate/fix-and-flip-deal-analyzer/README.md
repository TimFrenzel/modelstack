# Fix and Flip Deal Analyzer

**Category:** Real Estate

**Summary:** A straightforward analyzer to figure out if your house flip will actually turn a profit. I use this to run the numbers on purchase price, rehab costs and carrying fees so I know exactly what my maximum allowable offer should be before I get in too deep. 

**Structure:** The primary tab gives you slots for the purchase price, initial closing costs and a detailed rehab budget breakdown. Right below that, it automatically calculates your monthly holding costs based on the expected timeline of the project. The bottom section spits out your projected cash needed to close, your net profit and the final return on investment.
