# Short Term Rental Revenue Estimator

**Category:** Real Estate

**Summary:** Trying to underwrite an Airbnb or VRBO and need to figure out your exact margins? I built this short-term rental revenue estimator to dial in your expected cash flow instead of just guessing. It accounts for seasonality and platform fees so you don't end up upside down on the mortgage.

**Structure:** The main sheet collects your monthly revenue estimates based on projected occupancy rates and your average daily rate. Right below that, it subtracts your fixed costs (like the mortgage and insurance) and variable expenses (like cleaning and supplies) to automatically calculate your true net operating income.
