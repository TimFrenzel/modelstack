# Envelope Budget

**Category:** Personal Finance

**Summary:** This one is for someone who likes the envelope method but wants it to work with bank accounts and biweekly paychecks instead of cash. The useful part is the rollover rule. Some envelopes carry forward, others reset, and the workbook keeps that logic straight month by month. Nice fit for anyone who budgets by purpose and wants spending to draw down named buckets.

**Structure:** `Envelopes` is the reference sheet for envelope name, group, monthly target, rollover flag, priority, and annual target. `Funding` spreads 26 paychecks across the envelope columns and checks the remainder on each row. `Spending` is the transaction log tied to envelope dropdowns. `Monthly Balances` runs the real engine with `Starting`, `Funded`, `Spent`, and `Ending` for each month, including the yes or no rollover rule. `Dashboard` keeps the current envelope balance chart and the funded versus spent monthly trend in one place.
