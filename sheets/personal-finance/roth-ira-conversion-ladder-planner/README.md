# Roth IRA Conversion Ladder Planner

**Category:** Personal Finance

**Summary:** If you're trying to retire early and need to tap your tax-deferred accounts without taking a hit from early withdrawal penalties, a Roth conversion ladder is usually the smartest move. I built this planner to map out exactly how much you need to convert each year to cover your actual living expenses down the road. 

**Structure:** Drop your current portfolio balances, expected annual withdrawal needs, and assumed tax rates straight into the Inputs tab. The main Dashboard tab rolls that into a clean year-by-year schedule showing your conversions, the taxes you'll owe today, and the exact year that money actually becomes available to spend.
