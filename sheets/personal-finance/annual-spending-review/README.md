# Annual Spending Review

**Category:** Personal Finance

**Summary:** This is the kind of file I'd open once or twice a year when I want to look back instead of budgeting forward. It compares this year against the prior year, ranks the biggest dollar swings, and gives a rough next year forecast from the recent monthly run rate. Good for spotting drift that never feels obvious in the moment.

**Structure:** `This Year` and `Prior Year` each store monthly income, total spent, savings rate, and 20 spending categories across Jan to Dec. `Category Stats` calculates annual total, monthly average, high month, low month, standard deviation, and coefficient of variation for each category. `YoY Comparison` lines up this year and prior year totals, then adds dollar change, percent change, and rank by absolute move. `Forecast` uses the trailing Jul to Dec average unless the user types an override. `Summary` keeps the KPI block plus the monthly trend line and the category change bar chart.
