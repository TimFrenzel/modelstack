# Personal Balance Sheet

**Category:** Personal Finance

**Summary:** This workbook is more formal than a typical net worth tracker. I'd use it when I want quarterly snapshots, cleaner section totals, and a few health ratios that show whether the balance sheet is getting stronger or weaker. Good fit for someone who likes statement-style structure and wants more than one headline number.

**Structure:** `Balance Sheet` groups the current quarter into current assets, investment assets, fixed assets, current liabilities, and long term liabilities, with subtotals, total assets, total liabilities, and net worth. `Quarterly History` repeats that structure across eight quarters and adds quarter over quarter change rows at the bottom. `Ratios` calculates liquidity, debt to asset, solvency, savings rate, investment ratio, and housing ratio, then compares each one against benchmark ranges and assigns a status. `Changes` breaks the latest quarter move into prior balance, current balance, dollar change, percent change, and share of total net worth change, with top and bottom contributors highlighted. `Dashboard` brings together the net worth line with a simple trendline, asset mix chart, ratio chart of current values, and section level change chart.
