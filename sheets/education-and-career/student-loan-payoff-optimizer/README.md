# Student Loan Payoff Optimizer

**Category:** Education and Career

**Summary:** I put together this payoff optimizer to help you figure out the fastest way to get out of student loan debt. It lets you run the numbers on different payoff strategies like the debt snowball or avalanche so you aren't just guessing. Pretty handy if you've got multiple loans with different interest rates and balances.

**Structure:** The inputs section at the top handles your individual loan balances and interest rates, plus whatever extra cash you can throw at them each month. The main table rolls the calculations forward month by month. It's built to automatically stack your payments toward the highest impact loan based on the method you pick.
