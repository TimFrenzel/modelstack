# Restaurant Sales & Waste

**Category:** Business

**Summary:** Weekly sales and food waste tracker for a small restaurant called El Camino Kitchen. This one's useful if you're running a place where food cost creeps up and you can't tell whether it's over-prepping, spoilage, or menu pricing. The waste log forces you to write down what got thrown out and why, which is half the battle. Targets for food cost percent and weekly revenue sit right at the top so the numbers stay honest.

**Structure:** `Weekly Sales` tracks revenue by day across multiple weeks, with food cost, labor cost, and a few other line items. Target food cost percent (30%) and target weekly revenue sit at the top. `Food Waste` is a running log with week number, ingredient, quantity wasted, unit, estimated cost, and a reason column (over-prepped, didn't rotate, etc.). `Menu & Costs` lists each menu item with ingredient costs, portion size, sell price, food cost percent, and prep time, plus the target food cost and labor rate at the top.
