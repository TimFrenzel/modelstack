# Freelance Invoice Tracker

**Category:** Freelance and Side Hustle

**Summary:** I built this for a one-person web design shop (KM Digital) that needed to track invoices, see who's paying on time, and know how much is outstanding at a glance. It's not accounting software. It's a spreadsheet that answers "who owes me money?" and "which clients are worth keeping?" If you're freelancing and chasing 5-10 invoices a month, this covers it.

**Structure:** `Invoices` shows total outstanding and overdue at the top, then a full log with invoice number, date, client, description, amount, status, date paid, days to pay, payment method, late fee, and notes. `Client Summary` rolls up each client's total invoiced, total paid, outstanding balance, number of invoices, average days to pay, longest wait, a rating, and notes. `Rate Card` lists hourly rates by service type, plus project-based and retainer pricing with notes.
