# Freelance PnL Tracker

**Category:** Freelance and Side Hustle

**Summary:** Trying to figure out your exact profit at tax time is a nightmare, so I built this simple PnL tracker for independent contractors and freelancers. It logs your income and deductibles strictly by month. It's meant for folks who don't want to run heavy accounting software just to track a side gig.

**Structure:** You've got an Income tab and an Expenses tab where you just dump raw transactions as they happen. The Dashboard tab then aggregates those rows using basic SUMIFS formulas to give you a clean month-by-month profit breakdown. Nothing too heavy, just exactly what you need to see.
