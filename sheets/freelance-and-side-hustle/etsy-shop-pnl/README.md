# Etsy Shop PnL

**Category:** Freelance and Side Hustle

**Summary:** A P&L tracker for a small Etsy candle business called Tangled Root Studio. I'd use this if I were selling handmade goods online and wanted to see whether individual products actually make money once you account for shipping, platform fees, and raw material costs. It's got enough detail to spot which SKUs carry the margin and which ones don't, without turning into a full accounting system.

**Structure:** `Monthly P&L` runs four months of revenue, cost of goods, Etsy fees, shipping costs, ad spend, and other expenses down to monthly net profit with notes. `Order Log` has every individual order with order number, date, item, quantity, price, shipping charged versus actual ship cost, platform, and notes. `Product Costs` breaks each product into wax, fragrance, wick, container, label, and packaging costs, totals them up, and calculates margin against the selling price.
