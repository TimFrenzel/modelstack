# Options Wheel Strategy Log

**Category:** Investing

**Summary:** I couldn't find a clean way to track the options wheel strategy, so I built this log to keep everything straight. Tracks your cash-secured puts & covered calls step by step. Great for knowing exactly what your true breakeven is after collecting a few rounds of premiums.

**Structure:** There's a main tracking table where you drop in your trade details like strike price, expiration and premium collected. The columns on the right automatically figure out your cost basis if you get assigned and track your realized profit when the trade closes out. I left a few rows up top for a quick dashboard summary.
